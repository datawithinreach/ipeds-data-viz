## Admission-rate trends across schools

Across these eight Massachusetts private colleges, the share of applicants admitted stays within a fairly narrow band. Over 2022-2024, most institutions move by only a few percentage points year to year.

The sharpest declines show up at Boston University (14% in 2022 to 11% in 2024). Some schools are comparatively steady (for example, Harvard and Wellesley remain close to 3-5% and 14%, respectively), while Amherst trends upward (+2 percentage points from 2022 to 2024).

## Reading the lines

Because each line represents a different institution, slopes indicate relative change in selectivity level over time. Nearly flat lines suggest stable admission rates, while steeper slopes indicate faster movement across years.
