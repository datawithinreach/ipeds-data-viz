## What this shows

IPEDS reports a “percent admitted” metric for first-year applicants. The lines above track that value across four admissions cycles (2021–2024) for a small set of highly selective, non-Ivy universities. Many of these rates are flat year-to-year—so small moves (1–2 percentage points) can still represent meaningful changes in how competitive admissions were for that cycle.
