IPEDS’ Student Financial Aid (SFA) table reports the share of undergraduates receiving a Federal Pell Grant. Across this small set of highly selective, non-Ivy universities, the 2023–24 Pell share ranges from the mid-teens to just under 30%.

## Pell share vs average grant aid

Pell share is one indicator of the income mix of an undergraduate population, while the average grant aid amount summarizes how much grant funding (federal, state, local, institutional, and other sources) is awarded per recipient. Plotting them together shows how these two measures vary across this set in 2023–24.
